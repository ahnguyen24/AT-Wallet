2. SECURITY ALGORITHMS & PROTOCOLS
-----------------------------------------------------------
- Key Stretching: PBKDF2-HMAC-SHA512 (600,000 iterations)
- Data Encryption: AES-256-GCM (Authenticated Encryption for seeds)
- Password Hashing: Argon2id (MGF1 compliant)
- Digital Signatures: Ed25519 (EdDSA) for transaction integrity
- Multi-Factor Auth: RFC 6238 (TOTP) - Google Authenticator compatible
- Integrity: SHA-256 (WebCrypto API) for Secure Envelopes
- Anti-Replay: Unique Nonce tracking + 30s Timestamp Window
